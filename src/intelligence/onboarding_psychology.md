# Onboarding Psychology | The 60-Second Transformation

## Goal
Move the user from "Curious" to "Obsessed" in under 60 seconds.

## The "First Minute" Blueprint
1. **0-10s (Immersion)**:
   - Instant full-screen cinematic background video (muted) with ambient sound.
   - Headline: "Step into your new identity."
2. **10-30s (Micro-Win)**:
   - A 15-second "Interactive Teaser" story segment.
   - User completes ONE shadowing task or listens to ONE powerful sentence.
   - Reward: Immediate "Mastery Glow" feedback.
3. **30-50s (Identity Anchor)**:
   - Ask: "What kind of protagonist are you?" (Business, Philosophy, Daily Life).
   - Show a "Preview" of their future self in that category.
4. **50-60s (The Commitment)**:
   - "Your first chapter is ready. Start your streak today."
   - Set the daily reminder as a "Mission Call."

## Key Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Don't explain the features. Let the user hear the cinematic narration immediately.
- **Instant Momentum**: The first "Lesson" should be the first thing they see after login.
- **Premium Friction**: High-quality animations that make the user slow down and appreciate the design (Signals quality).
