# Social Clip & Viral Sharing Engine | Growth Blueprint

This system transforms internal learning moments into external social currency, driving organic growth through "Emotional Virality."

## 1. Social Clip Architecture (Task 1 & 3)
The engine generates three primary formats for social distribution:

### A. The Cinematic Reel (Vertical Video)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (TikTok/Reels/Shorts).
- **Visuals**: Dynamic AtmosphereLayer (Background) + Animated Kinetic Typography.
- **Audio**: Original story audio + Ambient "Cinematic Textures."
- **Key Feature**: "Breath-Synced Subtitles" that pulse with the speaker's rhythm.

### B. The Philosophy Card (Quote Engine)
- **Visuals**: Glassmorphism cards over "The Void" or "The Calm" backgrounds.
- **Typography**: Outfit (Bold) for the hook line, Inter (Light) for the subtext.
- **Branding**: Subtle "Cinematic English" watermark with identity rank (e.g., *Storywalker*).

### C. The Momentum Aura (Identity Sharing)
- **Visuals**: A circular glowing energy field representing the user's streak and identity progression.
- **Vibe**: "Non-cringe" prestige. It looks like a luxury watch face or a modern art piece.

## 2. Auto Clip Detection Logic (Task 2)
The AI (Story Weaver SW-1) flags segments during generation using the following "Viral Triggers":
- **The Paradox**: A statement that contradicts common sense (e.g., "Silence is the loudest message").
- **The Power Shift**: Dialogue where the protagonist gains psychological leverage.
- **The Vulnerability**: A moment of deep emotional honesty.
- **The Rhythm Peak**: A sentence with high phonetic impact and perfect cadence for shadowing.

## 3. Social Identity System (Task 6)
Sharing is tied to the **Identity Progression** hierarchy:
- **Silent Observer**: Sharing "The First Step" (Quietly aesthetic).
- **Speaker**: Sharing "The First Voice" (Audio snippets).
- **Protagonist**: Sharing "The Mastery" (Full cinematic clips).
- **Voice Architect**: Sharing "The Creation" (Customized story fragments).

## 4. Viral Growth Governance (Task 8)
Every shareable asset must pass the **Social Resonance Audit**:
1. Does this look like a "Premium Movie Clip"?
2. Does it provide value/insight even if you don't know the app?
3. Is it "Binge-worthy" or "Save-worthy"?
4. Does it avoid "App Ad" cliches (no "Download now" banners)?

## 5. Creator Mode Architecture (Task 7)
- **Moment Vault**: A storage system for user-flagged "Emotional Breakthroughs."
- **Export Pipeline**: A serverless function (e.g., Remotion or similar) to render high-quality MP4s from React components.
- **Custom Overlays**: Allow users to toggle "Aura Strength" or "Shadowing Waveforms" on their clips.

## 6. Mobile-First Share UX (Task 9)
- **Action**: "One-Tap Snapshot."
- **Feedback**: Haptic vibration + Cinematic "Camera Shutter" sound.
- **Destination**: Native share sheet targeting Instagram Stories/TikTok first.
