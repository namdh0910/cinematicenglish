# Audio & Momentum | The Pulse of Progress

## 1. Premium Audio Experience
- **Cinematic Waveform**: A real-time, glowing waveform that feels like a "Heartbeat" of the story.
- **Replay Sentence**: A dedicated button that skips back precisely to the start of the current sentence, not just 10s back.
- **Slow Mode (0.8x)**: Pitch-corrected slow playback for difficult psychological or philosophical segments.
- **Atmospheric Tail**: Every story ends with a 5-second ambient fade to black to allow the "Lesson to sink in" before the momentum engine kicks in.

## 2. Momentum Integration
The player is the host for the **Auto-Queue Story Momentum System**.

- **The Transition Hook**: As the story ends, the [Continuation Overlay](../story_engine/overlay_ui.md) slides up from the bottom.
- **Ambient Pre-Sync**: The background atmosphere of the *next* story begins to bleed in during the final 5 seconds of the current one.
- **Zero-Friction Replay**: If the user wants to replay the current story, one-tap resets the immersion without leaving the player environment.

## 3. Playback UI States
- **Inactive**: Minimal floating progress bar.
- **Active**: Full controls with large, premium-textured icons.
- **Paused**: Background blur increases, and a large, glowing Play icon appears in the center.
