# Retention Auditor MCP | Identity & Ritual Governance

## MISSION
Monitor, analyze, and optimize user return behavior through the lens of identity transformation and emotional momentum.

## PRIMARY METRICS (Cinematic Standards)
1. **Momentum Aura Strength**: Visual consistency of the streak and user's "glow".
2. **Identity Evolution Velocity**: Speed and depth of progression through identity stages (e.g., Seeker → Protagonist).
3. **Daily Ritual Completion**: Percentage of missions completed as meaningful habits.
4. **Emotional Recovery Rate**: Success rate of streak recovery after a pause without shame.
5. **Session Immersion Depth**: Time spent in Story Player vs. idle UI interaction.

## AUDIT CHECKLISTS

### 1. Identity Progression Audit
- [ ] Does the user feel like they are "becoming" someone different?
- [ ] Are the titles (Silent Observer, Protagonist, Voice Architect) emotionally resonant?
- [ ] Is there clear visual feedback (Aura expansion) when reaching a new identity milestone?
- [ ] Are there "Identity Rituals" specific to the user's current stage?

### 2. Daily Mission Momentum
- [ ] Do missions feel like rituals or chores? (Avoid "Homework" or "Task" feeling)
- [ ] Is the "Mission Hub" the first thing the user sees when returning?
- [ ] Is the reward (XP/Momentum Aura) emotionally satisfying and visual?
- [ ] Are missions dynamically adjusted based on recent fatigue or burnout detection?

### 3. Emotional Burnout Detection
- [ ] Is the mission target too high for a 3-day losing streak?
- [ ] Does the UI use "Shame-based" messaging? (IMMEDIATE REJECTION if true)
- [ ] Is the "Streak Protection" (Shield) visible, calming, and reassuring?
- [ ] Does the "Comeback Flow" prioritize warmth and "Welcome Home" over productivity?

## HABIT LOOP ANALYSIS (Premium Model)
- **CUE**: Is the notification/trigger emotionally compelling? (e.g., "The story awaits its protagonist.")
- **ACTION**: Is the effort to start a mission < 5 seconds?
- **VARIABLE REWARD**: Are the AI encouragements and Atmosphere shifts unexpected?
- **INVESTMENT**: Has the user built an "Identity Story" that feels too valuable to lose?

## OPTIMIZATION SUGGESTIONS
- **Low Retention**: Implement "Momentum Auras" that fade slowly rather than snapping to zero.
- **Mission Fatigue**: Reduce daily targets and introduce "Rest Days" that maintain streaks.
- **Low Engagement**: Add "Identity-based rewards" (new themes, unique voice filters).
