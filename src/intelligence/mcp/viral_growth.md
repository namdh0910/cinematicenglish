# Viral Growth MCP | Social Resonance Auditor

This auditor ensures every shared moment has the potential to go viral while maintaining the premium "Cinematic English" brand identity.

## 1. Visual Aesthetic Audit
- [ ] **Premium Lighting**: Does the clip use the "Cinematic Dark Mode" palette (#050508)?
- [ ] **Typography Hierarchy**: Is the main quote prominent and readable in a vertical feed?
- [ ] **Motion Polish**: Are transitions smooth (cubic-bezier) and intentional?
- [ ] **Brand Subtlety**: Is the branding "Prestige-first" (no loud logos)?

## 2. Emotional Resonance Audit
- [ ] **The "Save" Factor**: Would a user save this to their "Quotes" or "Inspiration" folder?
- [ ] **The "Share" Factor**: Does this make the sharer look "High-Value" or "Sophisticated"?
- [ ] **Identity Alignment**: Does the content reinforce the "Storywalker/Protagonist" transformation?
- [ ] **Context Independence**: Does the clip make sense and feel powerful without the rest of the story?

## 3. Social Engine Performance
- [ ] **Export Speed**: Can the UI generate the preview in < 500ms?
- [ ] **Vertical Optimization**: Are all elements centered and safe for TikTok/Instagram "UI overlays" (like the Like/Comment buttons)?
- [ ] **Interactive Hook**: Does the first 2 seconds of the clip capture immediate attention?

## 4. Viral Trigger Checklist
Check if the clip contains at least ONE of these:
- [ ] **Controversial Truth**: A bold claim about psychology or society.
- [ ] **Emotional Breakthrough**: A moment of intense vulnerability or clarity.
- [ ] **Aesthetic Mastery**: A visually stunning combination of kinetic type and atmosphere.
- [ ] **Technical Excellence**: A perfect demonstration of advanced English pronunciation/shadowing.

---
**REJECTION CRITERIA**:
- Generic "Success Quotes" (e.g., "Work hard, play hard").
- Cluttered UI or "Robotic" AI-generated text.
- Low-contrast typography that is hard to read on mobile.
- Any feeling of "Cheap Marketing."
