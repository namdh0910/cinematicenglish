# AI Story Auditor | Quality Governance Checklist

This checklist ensures all AI-generated content meets the "Cinematic English" premium standards.

## 1. Emotional & Cinematic Impact
- [ ] **The "Chill" Test**: Does the opening hook create a sense of intrigue or emotional resonance?
- [ ] **Subtext Check**: Is there more being said than just the literal words?
- [ ] **Atmospheric Alignment**: Does the writing match the assigned 'Mood' (The Void, The Pulse, The Calm)?
- [ ] **Character Voice**: Does the narrator sound like a distinct persona (e.g., The Mentor, The Protagonist) rather than a generic AI?

## 2. Language Immersion & Pedagogy
- [ ] **Naturalism**: Is the English used in a way a native speaker would actually use it in a dramatic context?
- [ ] **Vocabulary Context**: Are the 'Power Phrases' embedded in a way that their meaning is clear from the emotional situation?
- [ ] **Grammar Subtlety**: Are complex structures used naturally without feeling like "grammar exercises"?

## 3. Shadowing & Pronunciation
- [ ] **Rhythm & Flow**: Does the sentence have a clear musical beat?
- [ ] **Breath Markers**: Are there pauses [pause] or ellipsis (...) to guide the user's breathing?
- [ ] **Chunking**: Are the transcript lines broken into manageable pieces (5-10 words)?
- [ ] **Mouth-Feel**: Are the words chosen for their phonetic resonance and impact when spoken?

## 4. Bingeability (Momentum)
- [ ] **The Thread**: Does the story reference themes from the previous part of the chain?
- [ ] **The Cliffhanger**: Does the ending leave the user wanting to hear the next segment immediately?

## 5. Technical Metadata
- [ ] **Schema Compliance**: Does the JSON include all required fields (emotionalTone, difficulty, pacingProfile, etc.)?
- [ ] **Accuracy**: Does the Vietnamese translation capture the *emotional* intent, not just the literal meaning?

---
**REJECTION CRITERIA**:
- Any use of "Typical ESL" phrases (e.g., "In this lesson, we will learn...").
- Robotic or overly formal sentence structures.
- Flat, emotionless dialogue.
- Lack of rhythmic variety.
