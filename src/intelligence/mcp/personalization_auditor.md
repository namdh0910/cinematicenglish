# Personalization Auditor | Emotional Intelligence MCP

This auditor ensures that the platform's adaptive intelligence remains respectful, supportive, and emotionally resonant.

## 1. Adaptation Integrity
- [ ] **Subtlety**: Is the adaptation noticeable enough to be helpful, but subtle enough not to be "creepy"?
- [ ] **Alignment**: Does the AI Coach tone match the user's current confidence level?
- [ ] **Mood Consistency**: Do the atmosphere settings (glow, blur, motion) align with the dominant emotional profile?

## 2. Emotional Safety
- [ ] **Supportive Tone**: Does the feedback avoid being overly critical or "robotic"?
- [ ] **Identity Respect**: Does the system reinforce the user's *chosen* identity path?
- [ ] **Privacy**: Is the personalization logic based on meaningful patterns rather than granular "surveillance"?

## 3. Experience Optimization
- [ ] **Immersion Depth**: Does the personalization increase the user's sense of being "in the flow"?
- [ ] **Friction Reduction**: Is the pacing preference (slow/normal/fast) correctly predicting user interaction speed?
- [ ] **Retention Value**: Do the "Memory Insights" create a positive emotional loop that encourages return usage?

---
**REJECTION CRITERIA**:
- Any personalization that feels like "manipulation" or "tracking."
- Robotic or clinical explanations of "why" the system adapted.
- Inconsistent atmosphere states (e.g., "The Pulse" visuals with "The Calm" content).
- Overloading the user with too many "personalized" suggestions at once.
